WhatsApp Messaging Tutorial

Learn to send messages, photos, and voice notes to family and friends

0:00
15:00

What You'll Learn

  • Download and install WhatsApp on your phone
  • Set up your account with phone number verification
  • Send text messages to family and friends
  • Share photos and videos from your gallery
  • Record and send voice messages
  • Create group chats with multiple family members

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Download WhatsApp

Go to your phone's app store and search for "WhatsApp". Tap the green app with a white phone icon.

Step 2: Verify Your Number

Enter your phone number and wait for the verification code via SMS.

Step 3: Add Your Profile

Add your name and a profile picture so your contacts can recognize you.

Step 4: Start Messaging

Tap the green message icon to start a conversation with someone in your contacts.
